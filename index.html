Drgoblin.com was launched in 2015 after I picked up the domain during a $1 GoDaddy promotion. The original version featured a repeating texture of my WoW character, but its resemblance to the tectus.rocks site, which was popular in the WoW community during the Highmaul raid, led me to fully embrace the theme. I recreated the site with falling Drgoblins, echoing the original.

The site was first hosted by an individual running a free service in memory of his late brother or something, but the operation was later sold, likely to monetise the user base. So in 2017, Drgoblin.com moved to FreeHosting.com, where it remained until 2025. Now, 10 years after its creation, the site has been updated and migrated to GitHub Pages.
